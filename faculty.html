Faculty

Our Faculty comprises of Senior Airline Captains, Veteran Pilots and Qualified Flying Instructors to teach aircraft handling and approach to flying. All of them have more than three decades of aviation experience. Air Force Veterans from the Air Traffic Control and Meteorology will teach you Air Regulations and Met. Aerospace Engineers cum pilots’ will teach you Tech General subjects. RTR will be taught by Veteran Army Aviator with more than three decades of Aviation Experience. We have guest lectures from Pilots’ on aircraft handling and aerodynamics. We will also have visits to actual aircraft as part of the curriculum.

In short, there is an unbeatable faculty with decades of experience for you to learn about flying and pass the DGCA exams.
