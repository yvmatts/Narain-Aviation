Message From CEO

  • The importance of training was long imbibed during my Air Force days. The briefing podium in my first fighter squadron had the motto “YOU FLY LIKE YOU TRAIN.” This left an indelible impression on me as I grew up in the Air Force. I have seen it happen live. Well-structured training programs with the right faculty and advanced training methods produce outstanding results.
  • I have more than three decades of experience in aviation. I never came second in any professional course in the Air Force. I was adjudged the Best in Ground Subjects, Best in Flying, Best in Aerobatics and the Best Overall Student in the Qualified Flying Instructor’s Course in Flying Instructor’s School in Indian Air Force. I became a Cat A Instructor in my first attempt. Not many are able to do so. Many fail after repeated attempts. I was one of the youngest Air Force Examiner at merely 13 years of service.
